Projects

- Neural Style Transfer Web Application

As my bachelor project, I designed and implemented image style transfer using CNNs as a web application. This application gets two images from the user (content and style images) and outputs a painted image resulting from applying artwork style to the content image; this process consists of 3 phases. In the first and second phases, the application recreates the content and style images. To achieve this goal, I reconstructed the VGG19 network for three reasons. First, I did not need the last three layers of VGG19, which are for classification. Second, as I experienced, the AVG layers have better performance than Max pool layers in this application, and third, I wanted to get outputs from different layers in the process of reproducing the style image (I chose five different layers to get features of style). Using the VGG19, I extracted the images' main features as vectors and defined two loss functions. L_Content is the mean square error between the output of phase one and the content image, and L_Style is the mean square error of transformed version of stylized and style images (I used the gram matrix as a transformer). In the last step, I optimized the total weight of the two losses. I tried to compare different weights to find the best ratio of coefficients in general. For optimization, I compared the quality and performance of the Adam and L-BFGS algorithms. In the last phase, the high-frequency components have increased in the resulting image. To solve this issue, in each iteration of optimization, I pass the resulting image through a filter to calculate the high-frequency noises in the X-axis and Y-axis. I consider the sum of the squares of these values as the third parameter in the final loss function. This project's back-end section is based on Python (Django Framework), and the front-end is based on VueJs.

Supervisor: Dr. Nazerfard

Amirkabir University of Technology, Tehran, Iran. Spring-Summer 2020

Users can submit their information needs in the form of a query in both simple and advanced modes. News will be ranked and displayed on the results page based on their similarity to the given query. Also, a group of similar news will be displayed on the page of each news.
